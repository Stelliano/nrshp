SACOS History

How to join

As a result of the fact that the history of Non-Racial Sport has been suppressed, marginalised and completely neglected not only by the “new unified” codes but by SASCOC, Government institutions and related NGO’s, Academic Institution’s and journalists.

As a result, there are many previously “white only” sports bodies that do not believe that we played sport. This narrative feeds the journalists that continue to ignore the fact that we played sport and that we have great athletes and administrators that never get honored. We simply cannot accept this status because we have a very rich history and our struggles contributed to the freedom and democratic society we now enjoy.

On reflection we concluded that we would form a constituted body governed by the Laws of Partnerships and Voluntary Association Laws. This would ensure that the history that we develop cannot be marginalized any longer. The NRSHP is the only body of its kind in the Republic of South Africa dedicated to the development of the very rich history of Non-Racial Sport. Developed by members of the NRSHP who played Non-Racial Sport.

The NRSHP is a constituted body who’s members are the very people that played Non-Racial Sport under the auspices of the South African Council on Sport (SACOS). The NRSHP is also a registered Non-Profit Organisation (NPO), and a registered Public Benefit Organisation (PBO).

It then begs the questions why are you not a member if you played Non Racial Sport under the banner of SACOS.
